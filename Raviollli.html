Raviolli Arabeata

Raviolla Arabeata:

Ravioli is fresh Italian pasta typically stuffed with ricotta, meat, cheese, or vegetables, sealed between two thin pasta layers and served with tomato or cream sauce.

Ingredients:

  • Refined flour: 200gms
  • Eggs: 2 nos
  • Olive oil: 20ml
  • Salt: a pinch

Stuffings:

  • Ricotta/fresh/cream cheese: 150gms
  • Spinach (blanched & chopped): 100gms
  • Seasoning: as required
  • Fresh basil: 8–10 leaves
  • Egg: 1 no (for sealing)

Arabiata Sauce

A spicy tomato sauce made with garlic, olive oil, and red chili flakes. “Arrabbiata” means "angry" due to the heat of the chili.
